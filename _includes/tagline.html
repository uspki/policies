The General Services Administration is in the process of updating the Public Trust TLS Certificate Policy. Archived copies are available on this site in the below list. Send any questions to FPKI at GSA.gov.

  • Version 1.0 FINAL of this Certificate Policy was approved by the Federal PKI Policy Authority on May 9, 2019.
  • Version 1.0 DRAFT of this Certificate Policy was posted for final vote from the Federal PKI Policy Authority and stakeholders.
  • Version 0.4 of this Certificate Policy was updated for team reviews against the practice statements and development.
  • Version 0.3 of this Certificate Policy was updated for internal team reviews against the practice statements and development.
  • Version 0.2 of this Certificate Policy was open for review and modification during initial development and some testing of the Certification Authorities.
  • Version 0.1 of this Certificate Policy was opened for comment in September 2017 and closed on October 31, 2017.

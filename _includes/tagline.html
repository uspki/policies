Securing Connections to Public Federal Websites and Web Services

The General Services Administration is in the process of updating the Public Trust TLS Certificate Policy. Archived copies of the Federal Public Trust TLS Certificate Policy are available on this site in the below list until May 2023.

After May 2023, this site will redirect to the Federal PKI Policy and Compliance Audit Page which will host new Federal Public Trust TLS Certificate Policy and Practice Statements as well as the archived copies listed below. Send any questions to FPKI at GSA.gov.

  • Version 1.0 DRAFT of this Certificate Policy was posted for final vote from the Federal PKI Policy Authority and stakeholders.
  • Version 0.4 DRAFT of this Certificate Policy was updated for team reviews against the practice statements and development.
  • Version 0.3 DRAFT of this Certificate Policy was updated for internal team reviews against the practice statements and development.
  • Version 0.2 DRAFT of this Certificate Policy was open for review and modification during initial development and some testing of the Certification Authorities.
  • Version 0.1 DRAFT of this Certificate Policy was opened for comment in September 2017 and closed on October 31, 2017.
